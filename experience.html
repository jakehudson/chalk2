Experience

Chalk 2 has unparalleled experience in providing chase support to Unmanned Aircraft. The company also has a strong commitment to safety, with no accidents or incidents in over 25,000 flying hours. We have 15 years of experience chasing unmanned aircraft.

We Are Qualified.

Our full-time pilots and observers are trained by a U.S. Army Test Pilot in chase and formation, in support of unmanned aircraft.

All Chalk2 pilots have specific training in UAS lost-link and flight termination procedures.

We Are Flexible.

Chalk 2 pilots are qualified to support aircraft ranging from rotor craft to fixed wing, and missions ranging from surface to Flight Level (FL) 300. We also have experience with specialized missions, including night formation.
